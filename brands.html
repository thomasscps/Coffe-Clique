Camera Brands

Here are some of the most popular brands of camera makers on the market. We have listed some pros of each.

Canon

Has quite a few camera bodies to choose from both dslr and mirrorless.

Easy to navigate menus. Great for portraits and weddings.

Downsides are you have very limited third party lens options so lenses can get quite pricy. But you have quite a few lens options.

Sony

Great at taking moving shots. So perfect for car phtography and sports photography.

Leading innovator of cameras on the market.

They are more expensive compared to other brands.

Nikon

Long history and established brand of great cameras.

Great sensors that let you take wildlife and landscape photos at a high quality.

Panasonic

Best cameras on the market for video.

Recommended for film and content creators like vloggers/youtubers.

Fujifilm

Feels like you are shooting on film even though its digital.

Perfect cameras for street and travel blogging.

OM System

Great all around camera. Often best used for travel photography.

High selection of available lenses as you can use OM System, Panasonic, or third party lenses.

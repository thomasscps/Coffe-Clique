Camera Bodies

We are focusing on the most popular medium for photos and that is digital. The two technologies to keep in mind are DSLR and Mirrorless.

DSLR

DSLR cameras are what we are used to as a society when you think of a digital camera that is not just poin and shoot.

They use a mirror inside of the camera body to reflect light into your viewfinder.

Mirrorless

Mirrorless cameras are not something most of the public know about right now.

It is a newer technology that uses sensors to display an electronic image onto a screen or viewfinder.

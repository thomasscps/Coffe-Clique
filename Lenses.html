Types of Lenses

There are many different types of lenses you can purchase for your camera. Most introduction kits come with a decent lense.

But there are several ones that are recommended for beginner photographers.

Standard Lense The everyday lense.
The perfect lense for capturing things as you see them.
Wide-angle Lense A lense that you want to use when your subject is most important.
It makes the focal point of the photo seem larger in the shot.
Telephoto Lense This is the lense you want to use when your subject is far away.
Commonly seen used with folks taking pictures of the night sky.
Macro Lense The perfect lense for capturing detail of a small object.
Often seen with photos of small wildlife or bugs.

Editing Software

It is perfectly reasonable to take a great photo and use that. But most photographers will branch out to take their photos to the next level with software. Here are some of the most commonly used softwares to edit photos.

Adobe Lightroom

Adobe Lightroom is a great editing tool that most photographers use and start their journeys with.

You can easily edit photos and can even make presets. An example preset would be for taking a nature photo in the morning or taking a picture of a moving car at night.

You focus mostly on editing your color, lighting, and exposure rates of your image.

Adobe Photoshop

Often you will see professional photographers initially edit their photos in Lightroom before moving that edit into Photoshop.

It is a powerful tool that gives you much more control over your edits but it has a much steeper learning curve. Often you will see photos over edited.
